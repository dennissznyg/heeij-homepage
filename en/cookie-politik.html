Cookie policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

We keep this page simple and don't track you.

Does this site use cookies?

No. The Heeij site uses no tracking or marketing cookies. To understand whether our content works, we use cookieless web statistics (Cloudflare Web Analytics): it sets no cookies, does not identify or track individual visitors, and only shows us aggregate numbers like page views and referral sources. That's why it requires no consent.

So what happens when I sign up?

When you sign up for the waitlist, your email is sent to our signup platform Brevo (EU). This is described in our privacy policy. The confirmation emails you receive from Brevo may contain a small invisible pixel that measures whether the email is opened — that's part of the email, not of this website.

If this changes

If we ever add analytics or other cookies, we'll update this policy and ask for your consent first.
